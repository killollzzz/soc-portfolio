**1. Брутфорс (много неудачных входов)**

```spl
index=web_logs 
(uri="*/login*" OR uri="*/signin*")
method=POST
(status=401 OR status=403)
| bucket _time span=1m
| stats count by _time, client_ip, uri
| where count > 10
| table _time, client_ip, count
```

**2. SQL-инъекции**

```spl
index=web_logs
| eval payload = lower(coalesce(body,"") . " " . coalesce(uri,""))
| search payload="*union select*" OR payload="*or '1'='1*" OR payload="*information_schema*" OR payload="*drop table*" OR payload="*xp_cmdshell*"
| stats count by _time, client_ip
| where count > 1
| table _time, client_ip, uri, count
```

**3. XSS-атаки**

```spl
index=* ("<script>" OR "alert(" OR "onerror=" OR "javascript:")
```

**4. Сканирование портов**

```spl
index=*
| stats dc(dest_port) as ports by src_ip
| where ports > 50
```

**5. Доступ к админке / скрытым файлам**

```spl
index=* ("/admin" OR "/wp-admin" OR "/phpmyadmin" OR "/.env" OR "/.git" OR "/backup")
```

**6. Подозрительные POST-запросы (загрузка файлов, команды)**

```spl
index=* ("POST" OR "PUT") ("cmd" OR "exec" OR "upload" OR "shell")`
```

**7. Алерт на command injection, с поиском тега пентестера

```spl
index=web_logs
| eval payload = lower(coalesce(body,"") . " " . coalesce(uri,""))
| search (
    payload="*|*" OR payload="*;*" OR payload="*`*" OR payload="*&*"
    OR payload="*%7C*" OR payload="*%3B*" OR payload="*%60*"
)
| search (
    payload="* id *" OR payload="*;id*" OR payload="*|id*"
    OR payload="* ls *" OR payload="*;ls*" OR payload="*|ls*"
    OR payload="*whoami*" OR payload="*cat*" OR payload="*ping*"
    OR payload="*nc*" OR payload="*sleep*" OR payload="*wget*" OR payload="*curl*"
)
| where NOT match(header, "(?i)x-bug-bounty")
| table _time, client_ip, body, uri, method, status
```

**10. Алерт на SQL-injection

```spl
index=web_logs (body="*UNION SELECT*" OR body="*OR '1'='1*" OR body="*information_schema*" OR body="*DROP TABLE*" OR body="*xp_cmdshell*" OR uri="*UNION*" OR uri="*SELECT*"

**11. Алерт на сканирование директорий (404)

index=web_logs status=404

```

**12. Алерт на FLI

**LFI (Local File Inclusion)** — уязвимость, позволяющая читать локальные файлы на сервере через манипуляцию с путями.

```spl
index=web_logs
(
    uri="*../*" OR
    uri="*..\\*" OR
    uri="*/etc/passwd*" OR
    uri="*/etc/shadow*" OR
    uri="*boot.ini*" OR
    uri="*win.ini*" OR
    uri="*system32*" OR
    uri="*%2e%2e%2f*" OR
    uri="*%252e%252e%252f*"
)
| table _time, client_ip, uri, method, status
```

**13. Алерт на FTP Exfiltration

```spl
index=network (dst_port=21) 
| bucket _time span=5m
| stats count by _time, src_ip, dst_ip, dst_port
| where count > 3
| table _time, src_ip, dst_ip, dst_port, count
```


**14. Алерт на сканирование Spring Boot Actuator (через where like)

```spl
index=web_logs
| eval uri=lower(uri)
| where like(uri, "%/actuator/gateway/routes%")
| table _time, client_ip, uri, method, status
```
