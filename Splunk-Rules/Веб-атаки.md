**1. Брутфорс (много неудачных входов)**

```spl
index=web_logs (uri="*/login*" OR uri="*/signin*") method=POST status=200
| bucket _time span=1m
| stats count by _time, client_ip
| where count > 10
| table _time, client_ip, count
```

**2. SQL-инъекции**

```spl
index=* ("UNION SELECT" OR "' OR '1'='1" OR "information_schema" OR "DROP TABLE" OR "xp_cmdshell")
```

**3. XSS-атаки**

```spl
index=* ("<script>" OR "alert(" OR "onerror=" OR "javascript:")
```

**4. Сканирование портов**

```spl
index=*
| stats dc(dest_port) as ports by src_ip
| where ports > 50
```

**5. Доступ к админке / скрытым файлам**

```spl
index=* ("/admin" OR "/wp-admin" OR "/phpmyadmin" OR "/.env" OR "/.git" OR "/backup")
```

**6. Подозрительные POST-запросы (загрузка файлов, команды)**

```spl
index=* ("POST" OR "PUT") ("cmd" OR "exec" OR "upload" OR "shell")`
```

**7. Алерт на command injection, с поиском тега пентестера

```spl
index=web_logs 
(body="*|*" OR body="*;*" OR body="*`*" OR body="*&*" OR body="*%7C*" OR body="*%3B*" OR body="*%60*") 
AND (body="*whoami*" OR body="*cat*" OR body="*ping*" OR body="*nc*" OR body="*sleep*" OR body="*ls*" OR body="*id*" OR body="*wget*" OR body="*curl*")
AND NOT header="*X-Bug-Bounty*"
| table _time, client_ip, body, uri, method, status
```

**8. Алерт на XSS

```spl
index=web_logs 
(uri="*<script>*" OR uri="*alert(*" OR uri="*onerror*" OR uri="*onmouseover*" OR uri="*<img*" OR uri="*<svg*" OR uri="*%3Cscript%3E*" OR uri="*%3Csvg*" OR uri="*%22%3E*" OR uri="*%27alert*" OR uri="*javascript:*" OR uri="*document.cookie*")
OR 
(body="*<script>*" OR body="*alert(*" OR body="*onerror*" OR body="*onmouseover*" OR body="*<img*" OR body="*<svg*" OR body="*%3Cscript%3E*" OR body="*%3Csvg*" OR body="*%22%3E*" OR body="*%27alert*" OR body="*document.cookie*")
| table _time, client_ip, uri, body, method, status
```

**9. Алерт на CSRF
```spl
index=web_logs uri="*/change-email*" method=POST NOT referer="*твой_домен*"
| table _time, client_ip, uri, method, referer, status
```

**10. Алерт на SQL-injection

```spl
index=web_logs (body="*UNION SELECT*" OR body="*OR '1'='1*" OR body="*information_schema*" OR body="*DROP TABLE*" OR body="*xp_cmdshell*" OR uri="*UNION*" OR uri="*SELECT*"
| stats count by _time, client_ip
| where count > 1
| table _time, client_ip, uri, count
```

**11. Алерт на сканирование директорий (404)

```spl
index=web_logs status=404
| bucket _time span=5m
| stats count by _time, client_ip, uri
| where count > 50
| table _time, client_ip, uri, count
```

**12. Алерт на FLI

**LFI (Local File Inclusion)** — уязвимость, позволяющая читать локальные файлы на сервере через манипуляцию с путями.

```spl
index=web_logs
(
    uri="*../*" OR
    uri="*..\\*" OR
    uri="*/etc/passwd*" OR
    uri="*/etc/shadow*" OR
    uri="*boot.ini*" OR
    uri="*win.ini*" OR
    uri="*system32*" OR
    uri="*%2e%2e%2f*" OR
    uri="*%252e%252e%252f*"
)
| table _time, client_ip, uri, method, status
```
